ABOUT US

Grand Blue was born from a deep love for the ocean and a dream to share the hidden wonders of Mauritius with the world. What started as a small team of ocean enthusiasts grew into a trusted company, welcoming adventurers, families, and explorers from all over the globe.

From our very first boat trip, our goal has always been the same: to create meaningful memories at sea. Whether it's the thrill of a first scuba dive, the serenity of a catamaran cruise, or the joy of spotting dolphins in their natural home, we've dedicated ourselves to making every journey unforgettable.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for combining safety, hospitality, and adventure. With a passionate crew, certified instructors, and a strong respect for the marine environment, Grand Blue continues to bring people closer to the ocean.
